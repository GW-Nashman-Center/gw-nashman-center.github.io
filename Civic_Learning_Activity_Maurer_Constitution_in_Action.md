# Constitution in Action: Connecting Founding Principles to Current Events

**Elizabeth L. Maurer**

Instructor, Graduate School of Education and Human Development, Master’s in Museum Education program

Adapted from the Citizenship in the Nation Merit Badge Workshop developed and delivered by the National Museum of the United States Army (Fort Belvoir, VA)

Purpose: To help students understand how constitutional principles shape contemporary civic life through analysis of current events.

Activity Type: Group Discussion, Case Study, Peer Review, Presentation

Time: 65 minutes

Recommended Class Size: 12–30 students, working in small groups
